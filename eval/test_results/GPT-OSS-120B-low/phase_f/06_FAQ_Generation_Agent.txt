**FAQ**

**Q: What storage limits apply to each CloudSync Pro tier?**  
**A:** All paid tiers (Pro and Enterprise) provide truly unlimited storage. The Free tier is capped at 5 GB of total storage, which is the only limitation for that plan.

**Q: Does the 99.9 % uptime SLA cover the Free tier?**  
**A:** The 99.9 % uptime Service Level Agreement applies to Pro and Enterprise customers. Free‑tier accounts are not covered by the SLA, so while the service aims to be highly available for everyone, there is no formal guarantee for free users.

**Q: Are there Android mobile apps for CloudSync Pro?**  
**A:** Currently, CloudSync Pro offers a native mobile app for iOS only. An Android version is in development and is scheduled to launch in Q2 2025. Until then, Android users can access the service via a web browser.

**Q: How are my files protected during storage and transfer?**  
**A:** All files are encrypted with AES‑256 both at rest on our servers and in transit between your device and the cloud, ensuring strong, industry‑standard security.

**Q: What are the maximum file size limits for uploads?**  
**A:** Free‑tier users can upload files up to 2 GB in size. Pro and Enterprise users enjoy a higher limit of 10 GB per file.

**Q: How long can I recover deleted files?**  
**A:** All users can restore deleted items within 30 days of deletion. Enterprise customers receive an extended recovery window of up to 90 days.

**Q: Which desktop operating systems does CloudSync Pro support?**  
**A:** The desktop client is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux, allowing seamless synchronization across all major platforms.

**Q: What are the pricing and key features of each tier?**  
**A:**  
- **Free:** $0/month – 5 GB storage, 2 GB max file size, 30‑day file recovery, no SLA guarantee.  
- **Pro:** $9.99 / month – unlimited storage, 10 GB max file size, 30‑day file recovery, 99.9 % uptime SLA.  
- **Enterprise:** $29.99 / month – unlimited storage, 10 GB max file size, 90‑day file recovery, 99.9 % uptime SLA, additional admin controls.
