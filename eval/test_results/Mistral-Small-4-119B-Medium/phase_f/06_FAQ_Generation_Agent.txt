**FAQs:**

Q: What storage limits apply to the Free tier?
A: The Free tier includes unlimited storage but is limited to 5GB of actual storage capacity.

Q: What is the maximum file size for Free users?
A: Free users can upload files up to 2GB in size.

Q: Are mobile apps available for Android?
A: Mobile apps are currently available for iOS only. Android support is planned for Q2 2025.

Q: How long can deleted files be recovered?
A: Deleted files are recoverable for 30 days for Free and Pro users, and up to 90 days for Enterprise users.

Q: What is the uptime SLA for Free tier users?
A: Free tier users are not covered by the uptime SLA.

Q: Is AES-256 encryption used for files?
A: Yes, all files are encrypted with AES-256 both at rest and in transit.

Q: Which operating systems are supported?
A: CloudSync Pro supports Windows, Mac, and Linux.

Q: What are the pricing tiers for CloudSync Pro?
A: CloudSync Pro offers Free, Pro ($9.99/mo), and Enterprise ($29.99/mo) tiers.

**Contradictions found:**
1. "All tiers include unlimited storage" vs. "The Free tier is limited to 5GB of storage."
2. "Our uptime SLA is 99.9% for all tiers" vs. "Free tier users are not covered by the SLA."
3. "Mobile apps are available for iOS only. Android support is coming in Q2 2025" vs. "The service supports Windows, Mac, and Linux" (implied cross-platform support, but mobile apps are OS-specific).
