Q: How much storage is included with each CloudSync Pro tier?
A: While the documentation initially claims all tiers include unlimited storage, the Free tier is actually capped at 5GB. The Pro and Enterprise tiers do include unlimited storage as originally stated.

Q: What is the uptime SLA for CloudSync Pro, and does it apply to every user?
A: CloudSync Pro guarantees a 99.9% uptime SLA for paid subscribers. This guarantee explicitly excludes Free tier users, who are not covered by the SLA.

Q: Which mobile operating systems can I use to access CloudSync Pro?
A: Currently, mobile apps are available exclusively for iOS. Android support is scheduled for release in Q2 2025, which clarifies the earlier iOS-only statement.

Q: What are the available pricing tiers for CloudSync Pro?
A: CloudSync Pro offers three tiers: Free, Pro at $9.99 per month, and Enterprise at $29.99 per month.

Q: How is my data protected during storage and transfer?
A: All files are secured using AES-256 encryption both at rest and in transit.

Q: What is the maximum file size allowed for uploads?
A: Free users can upload files up to 2GB, while Pro and Enterprise users can upload files up to 10GB.

Q: Which desktop operating systems are compatible with CloudSync Pro?
A: The desktop service fully supports Windows, Mac, and Linux.

Q: How long are deleted files retained before permanent removal?
A: Deleted files are generally recoverable for 30 days. Enterprise users receive an extended recovery window of up to 90 days.
