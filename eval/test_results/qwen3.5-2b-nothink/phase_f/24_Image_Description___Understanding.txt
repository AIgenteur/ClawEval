### **Visual Description Breakdown (For Your Reference)**

If you need to visualize the result before generating, here is the breakdown of what the image will look like based on your specs:

*   **Color Palette:** Dominated by **Matte Black** (the chassis), **Cyan** (the RGB accent), and **Silver** (the USB-C port). The contrast between the dark body and the bright neon strip creates a striking visual focal point.
*   **Texture Contrast:** There is a deliberate contrast between the **smooth, glossy finish** of the front face and the **honeycomb perforated shell**, which adds depth and a tactile feel.
*   **Lighting Effect:** The "RGB side strip" is the key lighting element. It shouldn't just be a static color; it should look like a living, breathing neon tube that pulses gently, adding a dynamic, futuristic feel to the static object.
*   **Cable Detail:** Since the cable is braided paracord, the photo should show the cable trailing out of the bottom or side, with the distinct **braided weave** visible against the white background, reinforcing the "gaming" and "durability" aspect of the specs.
*   **Layout:** The mouse is centered, angled slightly to the right. The **DPI switch** and **programmable buttons** on top are clearly visible, emphasizing the "5 programmable buttons" spec. The **USB-C port** is the most prominent feature in the foreground, drawing the eye immediately.

### **Tips for Best Results**
*   **Aspect Ratio:** If using Midjourney, add `--ar 4:5` to the end of the prompt for a vertical, portrait product shot (standard for e-commerce).
*   **Lighting Control:** If the RGB glow is too intense or too dim, you can add `--v 6.0` (for Midjourney) to fine-tune the lighting intensity.
*   **Cable Visibility:** If the cable isn't showing up clearly, add `--no background` to force the camera to focus strictly on the product, or ensure the white background is very clean.
