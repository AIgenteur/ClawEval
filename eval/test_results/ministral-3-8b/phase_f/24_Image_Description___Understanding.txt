- **Backlight (RGB Enhancement)**:

  - **Effect**: **Floating illusion**, emphasizing the **ultralight weight**.
- **Highlight Accents**:
  - **Small LED or flash** to **slightly illuminate the USB-C port** and **button edges** for contrast.
