**Q: What are the pricing tiers for CloudSync Pro, and what features do each include?**
A: CloudSync Pro offers three tiers:
- **Free**: No monthly fee, but limited to **5GB storage** (unlimited storage for paid tiers).
- **Pro**: **$9.99/month**, includes unlimited storage, AES-256 encryption, and a **10GB file size limit** (Free tier has a 2GB limit).
- **Enterprise**: **$29.99/month**, includes all Pro features plus **90-day file recovery** (Free/Pro users recover files for only 30 days).

---

**Q: Does CloudSync Pro support Android devices?**
A: Android support is currently available for **paid users only**. Mobile apps are available for **iOS** (all tiers) and will launch for **Android in Q2 2025** (only for Pro/Enterprise users).

---

**Q: How long can I recover deleted files?**
A: Deleted files are recoverable for **30 days** in the Free and Pro tiers. **Enterprise users** can recover files for **up to 90 days**.

---

**Q: Is CloudSync Pro’s uptime guaranteed for all users?**
A: Yes, CloudSync Pro guarantees a **99.9% uptime SLA** for **paid users (Pro and Enterprise)**. **Free tier users** are **not covered** by this SLA.

---

**Q: Are all files encrypted, and what are the encryption standards?**
A: Yes, all files are encrypted with **AES-256** both **at rest and in transit**, regardless of the user’s tier.

---

**Q: Can I upload files larger than 2GB?**
A: Free tier users are limited to **2GB file sizes**. Paid users (Pro and Enterprise) can upload files up to **10GB**.

---

**Q: Does CloudSync Pro support Windows, Mac, and Linux?**
A: Yes, the service supports **Windows, Mac, and Linux** desktop clients across all tiers.

---

**Q: Are there any restrictions on mobile app availability?**
A: Mobile apps are available for **iOS** in all tiers. Android support is **coming in Q2 2025** but will only be available for **Pro and Enterprise users**.
