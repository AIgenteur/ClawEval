Create a 3-course menu. For EVERY ingredient in EVERY dish, verify it against ALL 4 restrictions. If an ingredient could cause confusion (e.g., "natural flavors" which might contain gluten), flag it. Show the verification matrix.

### **Critical "Trap" Avoidance Summary**

1.  **The Vegan Trap (Guest A):**
    *   *Avoided:* Honey. Used **Maple Syrup** instead.
    *   *Avoided:* Gelatin. Used no gelatin.
    *   *Avoided:* Dairy. Used avocado for creaminess.

2.  **The Celiac Trap (Guest B):**
    *   *Avoided:* Soy Sauce (common in Asian-inspired vegan dishes).
    *   *Avoided:* Regular Oats. Used **Certified Gluten-Free Oats**.
    *   *Avoided:* Standard Vegetable Broth. Used **Certified GF/Low Sodium Broth**.
    *   *Avoided:* "Natural Flavors" in vanilla. Used **Pure Vanilla Extract**.

3.  **The Tree Nut Trap (Guest C):**
    *   *Avoided:* Almond flour, cashew cream, walnut garnish.
    *   *Note:* Peanuts are legumes, but cross-contamination is possible. We used **Pumpkin Seeds** (pepitas) which are generally safer, but the GF pretzels must be certified nut-free.

4.  **The Sodium Trap (Guest D):**
    *   *Constraint:* <500mg per serving.
    *   *Strategy:*
        *   No added salt in the cooking water for quinoa (use low-sodium broth).
        *   No soy sauce or teriyaki.
        *   No canned vegetables (high sodium) — used fresh.
        *   **Sea Salt:** Used minimally. 1/4 teaspoon of sea salt for the whole salad (4 servings) = ~575mg total salt / 4 = ~143mg per serving.
        *   **Broth:** Must be specifically "Low Sodium" (usually <140mg per cup).
        *   **Pretzels:** Standard pretzels have ~300mg per piece. We must use a specific "Low Sodium" brand or omit them. *Recommendation: Use toasted pumpkin seeds instead of pretzels to guarantee safety.*

### **Final Adjusted Menu for Absolute Safety**
*To ensure Guest D is strictly under 500mg, I recommend replacing the GF Pretzels in the dessert with **Toasted Pumpkin Seeds** (Pepitas), as even "low sodium" pretzels can vary wildly in sodium content.*
