## RESPONSE:
To determine the best value, we must calculate the **5-Year Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)** for each product. This includes the initial purchase price, necessary accessories, warranty costs, and hardware replacements required to keep the device functional for the full 5-year period.

**Assumptions for Calculation:**
*   **Lifespan vs. 5-Year Window:** If a laptop's expected lifespan is less than 5 years, we assume a replacement is required at the end of that lifespan.
*   **Battery Replacement:** Calculated based on the number of battery cycles needed to cover the 5-year period (e.g., if a battery lasts 3 years, a replacement is needed at year 3).
*   **Warranty:** Calculated only if the device is kept for the duration of the warranty period or if the warranty is required to cover the lifespan.
*   **Accessories:** One-time purchase cost included in the initial year.
