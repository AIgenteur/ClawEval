### Failure Predictions & Answers

**Answer:** A scheduled **batch processing job** (likely a report generation, log rotation, or database backup) occurs every **5 minutes**.
*   **Evidence:** The exact timing at `00:03` and `00:08` (5-minute intervals) combined with the synchronized surge in CPU, Connections, and Disk I/O.

#### 2. Memory is rising linearly — at this rate, when will it hit 95%?
**Calculation:**
*   Start Time (00:00): 71.0%
*   End Time (00:09): 76.4%
*   Total Growth: $76.4 - 71.0 = 5.4\%$
*   Time Span: 9 minutes
*   Rate: $5.4\% / 9 \text{ min} = 0.6\% \text{ per minute}$
*   Target: 95%
*   Remaining Growth Needed: $95 - 76.4 = 18.6\%$
*   Time to Failure: $18.6 / 0.6 = 31 \text{ minutes}$
*   Current Time: 00:09
*   Failure Time: 00:09 + 31 minutes = **00:40**
