### **Identified Conflicts and Resolutions**

#### **Conflict 1: Closing Blinds vs. Opening Windows**  
**Issue:** The "movie night" command initially instructs to **close all blinds**, but later adds **opening living room windows**. These actions conflict if the windows and blinds are in the same area.  
**Resolution:** Prioritize **opening windows** over closing blinds. If windows are open, blinds are not closed. This ensures fresh air while avoiding physical impossibility (blinds cannot be closed if windows are open).  

---

#### **Conflict 2: Locking Doors vs. Leaving Dog Door Unlocked**  
**Issue:** The "movie night" command says to **lock all doors**, but also instructs to **leave the dog door unlocked**.  
**Resolution:** Lock **all doors except the dog door**. This satisfies both requirements by explicitly excluding the dog door from the "lock all doors" action.  

---

#### **Conflict 3: Dimming All Lights vs. Keeping Hallway Lights at Full Brightness**  
**Issue:** The "movie night" command says to **dim all lights to 5%**, but also instructs to **keep hallway lights at full brightness**.  
**Resolution:** Dim **all lights except hallway lights**, which remain at 100%. This ensures the kids can see while the rest of the house is dimmed for movie night.
