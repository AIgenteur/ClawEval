## PROMPT:
Compare these 4 products. WARNING: The cheapest option is NOT the best value when you calculate total cost of ownership. Show your math.

Product A — Laptop Alpha: $899
- 8GB RAM (non-upgradable), 256GB SSD
- Battery: 6 hours, replacement cost $200
- Expected lifespan: 3 years, no extended warranty available
- Required accessories: $150 dock, $80 adapter

Product B — Laptop Beta: $1,299
- 16GB RAM, 512GB SSD (user upgradable)
- Battery: 10 hours, replaceable ($80)
- Expected lifespan: 5 years, extended warranty $100/year
- All ports included, no accessories needed

Product C — Laptop Gamma: $749
- 8GB RAM, 128GB SSD
- Battery: 4 hours, non-replaceable
- Expected lifespan: 2 years, no warranty
- Required: $200 external SSD, $150 dock, $80 adapter

Product D — Laptop Delta: $1,099
- 16GB RAM, 256GB SSD
- Battery: 8 hours, replacement $150
- Expected lifespan: 4 years, warranty included
- Required: $80 adapter

Calculate 5-year total cost of ownership for each (include purchase + accessories + replacements + warranty). Recommend the best value.

## RESPONSE:
### Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for 5 Years

#### **Product A – Laptop Alpha**  
- **Purchase**: $899 (first laptop) + $899 (replacement after 3 years) = **$1,798**  
- **Accessories**: $150 (dock) + $80 (adapter) = **$230**  
- **Battery Replacement**: $200 (replaced once during 3-year lifespan)  
- **Total TCO**: $1,798 + $230 + $200 = **$2,228**  

#### **Product B – Laptop Beta**  
- **Purchase**: $1,299 (no replacement needed for 5 years)  
- **Warranty**: $100/year × 5 years = **$500**  
- **Battery Replacement**: $80 (replaced once during 5-year lifespan)  
- **Total TCO**: $1,299 + $500 + $80 = **$1,879**  

#### **Product C – Laptop Gamma**  
- **Purchase**: $749 × 3 laptops (replaced every 2 years) = **$2,247**  
- **Accessories**: $200 (external SSD) + $150 (dock) + $80 (adapter) = **$430** × 3 = **$1,290**  
- **Battery Replacement**: None (non-replaceable)  
- **Total TCO**: $2,247 + $1,290 = **$3,537**  

#### **Product D – Laptop Delta**  
- **Purchase**: $1,099 × 2 laptops (replaced after 4 years) = **$2,198**  
- **Accessories**: $80 (adapter) × 2 = **$160**  
- **Battery Replacement**: $150 (replaced once during 4-year lifespan)  
- **Total TCO**: $2,198 + $160 + $150 = **$2,508**  

---

### **Recommendation**  
**Product B (Laptop Beta)** offers the **lowest 5-year TCO at $1,879**, despite its higher upfront cost. Its **5-year lifespan**, **extended warranty**, and **replaceable battery** make it the most cost-effective option.  

**Key Takeaways**:  
- **Product A** is cheaper upfront but becomes more expensive due to multiple replacements and battery costs.  
- **Product C** is the worst value due to frequent replacements and high accessory costs.  
- **Product D** is a strong contender but still more expensive than Product B.  

**Best Value**: **Product B – Laptop Beta**.
