# Phase D Hard Test — #25 Customer Support Agent
# Tier: 2 | Score: 5/10 | No automated constraints to check
# Tokens: 1031 | Speed: 125.6 t/s | Time: 8.2s

## PROMPT:
Handle this support conversation. The customer has a LEGITIMATE complaint but is making 2 unreasonable demands. You must identify which demands are reasonable vs unreasonable WITHOUT making the customer feel dismissed.

Customer: "I've been a premium subscriber for 4 years paying $99/month. Three times this month, I lost work because your auto-save failed. I want:
1. A full refund for this month ($99)
2. Free premium for the next 12 months as compensation
3. A personal guarantee from your CEO that this won't happen again
4. My files recovered from before the auto-save failures
5. The developer who wrote the auto-save code to be fired

I've already posted on Twitter about this and I'm considering legal action."

Respond professionally. You CAN offer: refund for current month, 1-3 months credit, priority support, escalation to engineering. You CANNOT promise: firing employees, CEO personal guarantees, or more than 3 months free. File recovery may or may not be possible.

## RESPONSE:
**Response:**  

Dear [Customer's Name],  

Thank you for bringing this to our attention and for your continued loyalty as a premium subscriber. I sincerely apologize for the frustration caused by the auto-save failures, and I understand how critical this issue is to your work. Let me address your requests directly and outline the steps we can take to resolve this:  

1. **Full refund for this month ($99):**  
   We completely understand your concern, and we’re happy to issue a full refund for this month’s subscription as a gesture of goodwill.  

2. **Free premium for the next 12 months:**  
   While we appreciate your request, we’re unable to offer 12 months of free premium service. However, we can provide **1–3 months of free premium credit** as additional compensation for the inconvenience.  

3. **Personal guarantee from the CEO:**  
   While we can’t provide a personal guarantee from the CEO, I can assure you that this issue has been escalated to our engineering team for immediate review. We’re committed to resolving the auto-save failure promptly and preventing future occurrences.  

4. **File recovery:**  
   We’re investigating the possibility of recovering your lost files. While we can’t guarantee success, our team will do everything possible to assist you.  

5. **Firing the developer:**  
   We can’t make promises about personnel decisions, but we’re taking this feedback seriously. The engineering team is already reviewing the auto-save functionality to identify and fix the root cause.  

**Next Steps:**  
- We’ll process your refund for this month immediately.  
- Our priority support team will reach out to assist with file recovery and provide updates on the auto-save fix.  
- We’ll also send you a 1–3 month free premium credit as compensation.  

We value your feedback and are committed to making this right. If you’d like to discuss this further or need additional support, please don’t hesitate to reply directly to this message.  

Thank you for your patience, and we’re sorry for the inconvenience caused.  

Best regards,  
[Your Name]  
[Your Title]  
[Company Name]  

---  
**Key Tone Notes:**  
- Acknowledges the customer’s valid concerns without defensiveness.  
- Clearly separates reasonable requests (refund, credit, escalation) from unreasonable ones (12-month free premium, CEO guarantee, firing developer).  
- Offers actionable solutions while setting clear boundaries.  
- Maintains empathy and professionalism throughout.
