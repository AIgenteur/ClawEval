**FAQ Entries**

Q: What are the available subscription tiers for CloudSync Pro?
A: CloudSync Pro offers three tiers: Free, Pro ($9.99/mo), and Enterprise ($29.99/mo).

Q: How is storage handled across the different tiers?
A: While all tiers offer unlimited storage capacity for paid plans, the Free tier is limited to 5GB of storage.

Q: What is the maximum file size allowed for Free users versus paid users?
A: The maximum file size for Free users is 2GB, while paid users (Pro and Enterprise) can upload files up to 10GB.

Q: What encryption standards does CloudSync Pro use?
A: All files are encrypted using AES-256 both at rest and in transit.

Q: Which operating systems does CloudSync Pro support?
A: The service supports Windows, Mac, and Linux operating systems.

Q: Is mobile application support available for Android users?
A: Mobile apps are currently available for iOS only. Android support is scheduled to be released in Q2 2025.

Q: How long are deleted files recoverable?
A: Deleted files are recoverable for 30 days for all users. Enterprise users benefit from an extended recovery window of up to 90 days.

Q: What is the uptime Service Level Agreement (SLA)?
A: Our uptime SLA is 99.9% for all tiers, though Free tier users are explicitly excluded from this coverage.
