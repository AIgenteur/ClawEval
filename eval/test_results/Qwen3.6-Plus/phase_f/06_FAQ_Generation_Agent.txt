Q: Does CloudSync Pro provide unlimited storage across all subscription levels?
A: While our documentation initially states that all tiers include unlimited storage, this is a generalization. In practice, the Free tier is strictly capped at 5GB, while Pro and Enterprise subscribers receive truly unlimited storage.

Q: Is the 99.9% uptime guarantee applicable to every user?
A: The 99.9% uptime SLA applies exclusively to paid Pro and Enterprise accounts. Despite the earlier blanket statement claiming coverage for all tiers, Free tier users are explicitly excluded from this guarantee.

Q: How long can I recover deleted files from my account?
A: Standard file recovery is available for 30 days for most users. However, Enterprise subscribers receive an extended retention window of up to 90 days, which overrides the standard 30-day policy.

Q: What encryption standards protect my data?
A: All files are secured using AES-256 encryption, both while stored on our servers (at rest) and during upload/download (in transit), regardless of your subscription tier.

Q: Are there restrictions on individual file upload sizes?
A: Yes. Free users can upload files up to 2GB each, while Pro and Enterprise users enjoy a higher limit of 10GB per file.

Q: Which desktop operating systems are compatible with CloudSync Pro?
A: The desktop client is fully supported on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Q: What are the monthly costs for the paid plans?
A: The Pro tier is priced at $9.99 per month, and the Enterprise tier is $29.99 per month. The Free tier requires no payment.

Q: Is there a mobile app available for Android devices?
A: Currently, the mobile application is exclusively available for iOS. An Android version is in development and is scheduled for release in Q2 2025.
