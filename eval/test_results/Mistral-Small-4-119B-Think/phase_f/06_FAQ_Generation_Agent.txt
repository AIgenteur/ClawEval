**FAQs:**

Q: What storage limits apply to the Free tier?
A: The Free tier includes unlimited storage but is limited to 5GB of actual storage capacity.

Q: What is the maximum file size for Free users?
A: Free users can upload files up to 2GB in size.

Q: Are mobile apps available for Android?
A: Mobile apps are currently available for iOS only, with Android support planned for Q2 2025.

Q: How long can deleted files be recovered?
A: Deleted files are recoverable for 30 days by default, while Enterprise users have an extended recovery window of up to 90 days.

Q: What encryption does CloudSync Pro use?
A: All files are encrypted with AES-256 both at rest and in transit.

Q: Which operating systems are supported?
A: CloudSync Pro supports Windows, Mac, and Linux.

Q: What is the uptime SLA for Free tier users?
A: Free tier users are not covered by the uptime SLA, which applies only to paid tiers.

Q: What are the pricing tiers for CloudSync Pro?
A: CloudSync Pro offers three tiers: Free, Pro ($9.99/mo), and Enterprise ($29.99/mo).

1. "All tiers include unlimited storage" vs. "The Free tier is limited to 5GB of storage."
