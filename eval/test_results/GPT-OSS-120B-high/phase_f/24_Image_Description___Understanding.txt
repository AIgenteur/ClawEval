**Overall Concept**  
A clean, high‑end “hero” shot that makes the mouse look like a piece of precision‑engineered hardware. The composition is tight, the focus razor‑sharp on the most distinctive features (the cyan‑glowing side‑strip and the honey‑comb shell), while the rest of the mouse gently falls into a shallow depth‑of‑field blur. The lighting is crisp and directional, giving the matte black surfaces a subtle satin sheen and the brushed‑paracord cable a tactile, woven texture. The background is a seamless, dark‑gradient surface that never competes with the product but adds a faint, high‑tech carbon‑fiber hint for context.

---

### 1. Camera & Lens Settings (for the prompt)

- **Camera:** Full‑frame DSLR or mirrorless (e.g., Sony α7R IV, Canon 5D Mark IV)  
- **Lens:** 85 mm macro prime (or 100 mm macro)  
- **Aperture:** f/2.8 – f/3.2 (shallow DOF, smooth bokeh)  
- **Shutter Speed:** 1/125 s (to freeze any tiny dust particles)  
- **ISO:** 100 – 200 (clean noise‑free image)  
- **Focus Point:** Single‑point AF locked on the left‑hand side‑strip where the cyan LED glows.  
- **White Balance:** Daylight (5600 K) – slightly cooler to keep the cyan hue vivid.  

---

### 2. Pose & Angle

- **Orientation:** The mouse is angled **30° upward** from the horizontal plane (so the top surface is partially visible) and **45° to the left** (showing the left side where the RGB strip runs).  
- **Tilt:** The front of the mouse is tilted **10° toward the camera** to expose the USB‑C charging port without hiding it.  
- **Distance:** The camera is positioned about **30 cm** from the mouse, filling the frame horizontally from the left edge of the side‑strip to the right edge of the thumb‑rest.  
- **Depth of Field:** The front‑most part of the side‑strip is razor‑sharp; the rear of the mouse (the right side and the cable) gently blurs, emphasizing the illuminated strip.

---

### 3. Lighting Setup

| Light | Position | Modifier | Purpose |
|-------|----------|----------|---------|
| **Key Light** | 45° left‑front, 2 ft above the mouse | Large softbox (12″ × 12″) with a slight grid | Provides soft, even illumination on the matte black body, revealing the satin texture and the honey‑comb perforations without harsh specular highlights. |
| **Fill Light** | 45° right‑front, 1 ft lower than key | Small octabox (8″) with a diffusion panel, set at 1/4 power | Lifts shadows on the right side, ensuring the mouse’s ergonomic curves are visible while keeping the overall contrast high‑tech. |
| **Rim / Edge Light** | Directly behind, 5 ft away, slightly to the left | Narrow barn doors with a cool‑white LED panel (5600 K) | Creates a thin, bright rim around the outer silhouette, accentuating the mouse’s sleek profile and the thin side‑strip. |
| **Accent Light (LED Strip Emphasis)** | Integrated into the side‑strip itself (the mouse’s built‑in cyan LEDs) | No external source – the strip is turned on at full brightness | The cyan glow spills softly onto the adjacent matte surface, producing a subtle “halo” that highlights the honey‑comb cells and the brushed‑paracord cable. |
| **Background Light** | Low, wide‑angle LED panel aimed at the backdrop | Soft diffusion, set to a very low intensity | Gives the dark gradient a faint, even illumination so the background never looks completely black, preventing a “hole‑in‑the‑wall” effect. |

- **Light Temperature:** All lights are balanced to daylight (5600 K) except the rim light, which is set to a cooler 6000 K to keep the cyan strip pop‑pable.  
- **Shadows:** Soft, elongated shadows fall to the right‑rear of the mouse, adding depth without obscuring details.  
- **Specular Highlights:** Minimal; the matte black finish should show only a whisper of highlight on the raised edges of the honey‑comb cells and the thumb‑rest ridge.

---

### 4. Background & Surface

- **Backdrop:** A seamless matte black to charcoal gradient (top: #0A0A0A, bottom: #1C1C1C) with a **subtle carbon‑fiber weave** texture at 2 % opacity—just enough to be perceived under close inspection but not to distract.  
- **Support Surface:** A **large, flat, matte‑black acrylic sheet** (roughly 12″ × 12″) placed on a low‑profile aluminum stand. The sheet has a **slight matte‑gloss** finish that reflects a faint, soft halo from the rim light, giving the mouse a “floating” feel.  
- **Reflection:** A **thin, diffused reflection** of the mouse appears on the acrylic surface, about 10 % opacity, to ground the product without creating a mirror‑like glare.

---

### 5. Detailed Visual Elements

#### a. Body & Finish
- **Material:** CNC‑machined ABS with a **matte‑satin coating**. The surface feels like brushed aluminum but is deep black, absorbing 85 % of ambient light.  
- **Texture:** No gloss; micro‑fine grain visible only under close‑up, giving a tactile “soft‑touch” impression.  
- **Ergonomics:** Slightly curved left‑hand side, a subtle thumb‑rest ridge, and a gently flared rear for palm support.

#### b. Honey‑comb Shell
- **Pattern:** Hexagonal cells, each ~2 mm across, arranged in a tight, uniform grid covering the entire top surface and the left side.  
- **Depth:** Cells are **0.8 mm deep**, creating a faint shadow line that catches the key light, emphasizing the perforated design.  
- **Edge Treatment:** The outermost cells are beveled, giving a clean, crisp edge that catches the rim light.

#### c. RGB Side‑Strip
- **Location:** Runs the full length of the left side, **2 mm wide**, recessed just 0.2 mm into the shell so the LEDs are hidden but the light diffuses through a frosted silicone cover.  
- **Color:** Pure cyan (#00FFFF) at 100 % brightness, producing a **soft, even glow** that gently illuminates the adjacent honey‑comb cells.  
- **Glow Effect:** A faint cyan halo (~3 mm radius) spreads onto the matte black surface, creating a “neon‑edge” look.

#### d. USB‑C Charging Port
- **Placement:** Centered on the front face, **3 mm from the bottom edge**, flush with the shell.  
- **Appearance:** Small, matte‑black insert with a **slightly recessed, polished stainless‑steel lip** that catches a tiny specular highlight.  
- **Detail:** The port’s internal pins are visible as a faint, dark‑gray line when the key light grazes it.

#### e. Buttons & Switches
- **Left Click / Right Click:** Slightly concave, matte‑black with a **micro‑embossed “click” icon** (tiny raised dot) for tactile feedback.  
- **Side Buttons (2):** Positioned on the left side, just above the thumb‑rest, each **3 mm × 5 mm**, with a subtle raised ridge for the index finger.  
- **DPI Switch Button:** Small, circular, **2 mm diameter**, located on the top surface near the scroll wheel, with a **laser‑etched “DPI” label** in silver. The button is slightly raised (0.3 mm) and has a **soft‑touch rubberized coating** that contrasts faintly with the matte body.  
- **Scroll Wheel:** A **hollow, CNC‑machined ring** with a **transparent cyan‑tinted bearing** that subtly reflects the side‑strip’s glow.

#### f. Detachable Cable
- **Type:** Braided paracord, **2 mm diameter**, **120 mm long** when fully extended.  
- **Color Scheme:** Dark gunmetal base with **orange‑red accent threads** woven throughout, giving a tactical look.  
- **Connector:** Small, right‑angle USB‑C plug with a **matte‑black housing** and a **tiny cyan LED** that mirrors the side‑strip when the mouse is powered.  
- **Presentation:** The cable is **coiled loosely** beside the mouse, with the first few loops visible, showing the texture of the braid. The coil is placed on the left side of the mouse, partially in the shallow DOF blur, hinting at its flexibility without stealing focus.

#### g. Weight Indicator (Optional Detail)
- **Marking:** A tiny **“67 g”** laser‑etched label on the underside of the mouse, near the rear, barely visible but discernible under the key light’s grazing angle.

---

### 6. Post‑Processing Notes (for the prompt)

- **Color Grading:** Keep the black true, with a slight boost to the cyan channel (+15) to make the side‑strip pop.  
- **Contrast:** Increase local contrast on the honey‑comb edges to accentuate depth.  
- **Sharpening:** Apply selective sharpening to the side‑strip and the USB‑C port, leaving the background soft.  
- **Noise Reduction:** Minimal, as ISO is low; preserve fine texture of the matte finish.  
- **Vignette:** Very subtle (‑0.2 EV) to draw the eye toward the center of the mouse.  
- **Bokeh:** Smooth, circular, with a faint cyan tint from the side‑strip’s spill.
