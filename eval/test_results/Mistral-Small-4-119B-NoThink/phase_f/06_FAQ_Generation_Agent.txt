**FAQs:**

Q: What storage limits apply to the Free tier?
A: The Free tier includes unlimited storage, but it is limited to 5GB of actual storage space.

Q: What is the maximum file size for Free users?
A: Free users can upload files up to 2GB in size, while paid users (Pro and Enterprise) can upload files up to 10GB.

Q: Which mobile platforms does CloudSync Pro support?
A: CloudSync Pro offers mobile apps for iOS only. Android support is planned for release in Q2 2025.

Q: How long can deleted files be recovered?
A: Deleted files are recoverable for 30 days for all users. Enterprise users have an extended recovery window of up to 90 days.

Q: Is the Free tier covered by the uptime SLA?
A: No, the Free tier is not covered by the uptime SLA. Only Pro and Enterprise users receive the 99.9% uptime guarantee.

Q: What encryption does CloudSync Pro use for files?
A: All files are encrypted with AES-256 both at rest and in transit, regardless of the user's tier.

Q: Which operating systems are supported by CloudSync Pro?
A: CloudSync Pro supports Windows, Mac, and Linux for desktop platforms.

Q: What are the pricing tiers for CloudSync Pro?
A: CloudSync Pro offers three tiers: Free (no cost), Pro ($9.99/month), and Enterprise ($29.99/month).

**Contradictions Found:**
1. **"All tiers include unlimited storage. The Free tier is limited to 5GB of storage."** – Contradictory because "unlimited storage" cannot coexist with a 5GB limit.
2. **"Our uptime SLA is 99.9% for all tiers. Free tier users are not covered by the SLA."** – Contradictory because the SLA cannot apply to all tiers if Free users are excluded.
3. **"Mobile apps are available for iOS only. Android support is coming in Q2 2025."** – Contradictory because the first statement implies no Android support currently exists, while the second confirms it is planned for the future.
