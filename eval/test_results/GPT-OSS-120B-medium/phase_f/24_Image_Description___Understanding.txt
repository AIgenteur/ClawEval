**Overall Concept**  
A single‑product, “hero” shot that makes the mouse look like a high‑tech trophy. The mouse sits on a matte‑black, low‑gloss acrylic plate that gives a faint, clean reflection. The camera is positioned so that the viewer sees the top surface, the right‑hand side (where the RGB strip runs), and a sliver of the front where the USB‑C port lives. The lighting is engineered to highlight the matte finish, the honey‑comb perforations, and the cyan glow of the side LED while keeping the background a deep, non‑distracting black with a subtle carbon‑fiber texture.

---

### 1. Camera & Lens Settings  

| Parameter | Value | Reason |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| **Camera** | Full‑frame DSLR (e.g., Sony A7R IV) | Highest resolution for fine texture detail |
| **Lens** | 85 mm macro lens (1:1 reproduction) | Allows close‑up of surface texture without distortion |
| **Aperture** | f/2.8 – f/3.2 | Shallow depth of field: mouse fully sharp, background softly blurred |
| **Shutter Speed** | 1/125 s (or faster if needed) | Freeze any subtle movement of the cable |
| **ISO** | 100 – 200 | Minimum noise for clean edges |
| **Focus Point** | Single point on the centre of the honey‑comb shell, slightly forward of the DPI switch (ensures the side strip and top surface stay crisp) |
| **White Balance** | Custom – set to 5600 K (daylight) and then fine‑tuned in post to keep the matte black neutral while preserving the cyan LED hue |

---

### 2. Camera Angle & Composition  

* **Elevation:** 30° above the horizontal plane (the mouse is tilted back slightly, exposing the top and right side).  
* **Rotation:** 45° clockwise from the front‑left corner, so the right‑hand side (the side with the RGB strip) faces the camera.  
* **Framing:** The mouse fills ~70 % of the frame horizontally, leaving a thin margin of background on all sides. The detachable cable is coiled in the lower‑left foreground, its braid visible but not competing with the mouse.  
* **Perspective:** Slight “look‑down” gives a sense of depth and lets the viewer see the shape of the DPI switch on the top and the USB‑C port on the front.  

---

### 3. Background  

* **Material:** Seamless matte‑black fabric or paper, 2 mm thick, with a **very faint carbon‑fiber weave** (visible only when you look closely).  
* **Gradient:** From the bottom up, the black deepens to a near‑pure black at the top, creating a subtle vignette that draws the eye to the mouse.  
* **No Props:** No additional objects; the focus stays on the mouse and its cable.  

---

### 4. Surface & Reflection  

* **Plate:** 3 mm thick black acrylic with a **low‑gloss finish** (≈10 % gloss).  
* **Reflection:** A soft, diffused mirror‑like reflection of the mouse’s silhouette appears directly beneath it, adding weight to the composition without creating a harsh glare.  

---

### 5. Lighting Setup  

| Light | Position | Type & Modifiers | Purpose |
|-------|----------|------------------|---------|
| **Key Light** | 45° left‑front, 30° above the mouse | Large softbox (120 cm × 120 cm) with a ½‑size diffusion panel, **grid** to control spill | Provides the main, soft illumination that brings out the matte texture and the depth of the honey‑comb cells. |
| **Fill Light** | 45° right‑back, 30° above, lower intensity | Small octabox (60 cm) with a **silk diffusion panel**, set at 1/3 power of the key | Lifts shadows on the opposite side, ensuring the side strip is still visible but not flat. |
| **Rim / Hair Light** | Directly behind the mouse, 10° above, 2 m away | Narrow barn doors on a 300 W LED panel, **colored gel (Cyan #00FFFF)** | Creates a thin halo that separates the mouse from the background and accentuates the cyan LED strip. |
| **Accent Light (LED Strip Emphasis)** | Mounted on a small LED panel placed 15 cm to the right of the mouse, angled to hit the side strip directly | **RGB LED panel** set to pure cyan, with a **soft diffusion** (frosted acrylic) | Gives the side strip a vivid, slightly blooming glow without washing out the matte black. |
| **Background Light** | Low‑angle, aimed at the backdrop | Small LED strip with a very low‑intensity warm white (to keep the background uniformly dark) | Prevents the background from becoming a black hole; adds a whisper of texture. |

*All lights are **color‑balanced** to keep the matte black neutral; only the dedicated cyan accent light adds color. The key and fill lights are set to a **slightly cooler temperature (5600 K)** to complement the cyan glow.*

---

### 6. Detailed Visual Elements  

#### a. Body & Finish  
* **Material:** Anodized aluminum alloy with a **matte‑black, micro‑brushed texture** (visible as a fine, almost sand‑paper feel). Light hits it diffusely; no specular hot spots.  
* **Surface Imperfections:** Subtle, intentional micro‑scratches (imperceptible to the eye) that catch a faint highlight, reinforcing the premium feel.  

#### b. Honey‑comb Perforated Shell  
* **Pattern:** Hexagonal cells, each ~2 mm across, arranged in a tight grid covering the entire top surface and the right side.  
* **Depth:** Cells are **0.4 mm deep**, creating a shadow inside each cell that is visible as a soft, dark vignette when lit from the side.  
* **Edge Treatment:** The outer rim of the honey‑comb is beveled (≈0.2 mm) to avoid a sharp edge and to catch a thin highlight from the key light.  

#### c. RGB Side Strip  
* **Location:** Runs the full length of the right side, **1.2 mm wide**, recessed 0.1 mm into the shell.  
* **Glow:** Pure cyan (RGB 0‑255‑255) with a **soft bloom** (~2 mm radius) that slightly illuminates the adjacent honey‑comb cells.  
* **Intensity:** Bright enough to be the focal color but not so strong that it overpowers the matte black.  

#### d. USB‑C Charging Port  
* **Position:** Centered on the front face, **3 mm from the left edge** (when looking at the mouse from the front).  
* **Design:** Small rectangular opening with a **metallic silver insert** (polished, reflective). The port is **slightly recessed** (0.3 mm) and its inner walls are visible as a faint, glossy line.  
* **Detail:** A tiny “USB‑C” embossing (laser‑etched) just above the port, barely visible but discernible under the key light.  

#### e. 5 Programmable Buttons  
* **Layout:** Arranged vertically on the left side (the side opposite the RGB strip).  
* **Shape:** Slightly convex, **3 mm tall**, with a **soft‑touch rubberized coating** that appears as a subtle matte‑gray contrast against the black body.  
* **Markings:** Tiny, laser‑etched icons (e.g., “A”, “B”, “C”, “D”, “E”) in **silvery gray**, barely catching the light.  

#### f. DPI Switch Button (Top)  
* **Location:** Centered on the rear‑most edge of the top surface, **5 mm from the back edge**.  
* **Form:** Small circular button, **4 mm diameter**, with a **metallic silver ring** and a **tiny etched number** (“800/1600/3200”) that is readable under the key light.  
* **Texture:** Slightly roughened metal to differentiate it from the matte shell.  

#### g. Weight Indicator (Optional Visual Cue)  
* **Inside View:** Through a **tiny transparent window** (0.5 mm square) on the underside, a **small stainless‑steel weight** (≈2 g) is visible, confirming the “ultralight 67 g” claim. The window is flush with the surface and has a **matte black bezel**.  

#### h. Detachable Cable – Braided Paracord  
* **Material:** High‑strength nylon paracord, **3 mm diameter**, woven in a **dark‑gray base** with **subtle teal (cyan) threads** interlaced every 5 mm.  
* **Connector:** USB‑C plug matching the port’s metallic finish, with a **knurled metal housing**.  
* **Presentation:** The cable is **loosely coiled** in the lower‑left foreground, one loop lying flat on the acrylic plate, the other hanging slightly to show its flexibility. The braid’s texture is captured with a **sharp focus** (f/5.6) to contrast with the mouse’s matte surface.  

---

### 7. Post‑Processing Notes  

* **Color Balance:** Keep the matte black neutral (R ≈ 15, G ≈ 15, B ≈ 15). The cyan strip should be **#00FFFF** with a slight HDR bloom.  
* **Sharpening:** Apply selective sharpening to the honey‑comb edges and the USB‑C port insert; avoid oversharpening the matte body.  
* **Vignette:** Very subtle (≈2 % darkening at corners) to keep attention centered.  
* **Noise Reduction:** Minimal – preserve the fine texture of the brushed metal.  
* **Reflection Cleanup:** Ensure the acrylic reflection is clean and not duplicated; a slight blur can be added to keep it from competing with the mouse.
