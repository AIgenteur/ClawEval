**FAQ**

**Q: What are the different CloudSync Pro plans and their monthly costs?**  
**A:** CloudSync Pro offers three tiers: the Free tier (no cost), the Pro tier at $9.99 per month, and the Enterprise tier at $29.99 per month.

**Q: How much storage does each tier provide?**  
**A:** The Free tier is limited to 5 GB of storage. Both the Pro and Enterprise tiers provide “unlimited” storage, meaning there is no preset cap on the total amount of data you can store. The term “unlimited” applies only to the paid plans; the Free plan’s 5 GB limit is a deliberate restriction.

**Q: Is there a limit on the size of individual files I can upload?**  
**A:** Yes. Free‑tier users can upload files up to 2 GB each, while paid (Pro and Enterprise) users can upload files up to 10 GB each. The “unlimited storage” description refers to the aggregate amount of data you can keep, not to the size of any single file.

**Q: Which operating systems are supported by CloudSync Pro?**  
**A:** Desktop clients are available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Mobile apps are currently available for iOS only; an Android app is slated for release in Q2 2025.

**Q: How long can I recover deleted files?**  
**A:** All users can restore deleted items within 30 days of deletion. Enterprise customers enjoy an extended recovery window of up to 90 days.

**Q: What encryption does CloudSync Pro use for my data?**  
**A:** All files are encrypted with AES‑256 both at rest and in transit, ensuring strong protection regardless of plan.

**Q: What uptime guarantee does CloudSync Pro provide?**  
**A:** We commit to a 99.9 % uptime SLA for all paid tiers (Pro and Enterprise). The Free tier is not covered by this SLA, so while we strive for the same reliability, there is no formal guarantee for free users.

**Q: If I’m on the Free tier, am I still protected by the SLA?**  
**A:** No. The SLA applies only to paid subscriptions. Free‑tier users benefit from the same infrastructure but do not receive the contractual uptime guarantee.

### Contradictions Identified in the Documentation

1. **“All tiers include unlimited storage” vs. “The Free tier is limited to 5 GB of storage.”**  
2. **“Our uptime SLA is 99.9 % for all tiers” vs. “Free tier users are not covered by the SLA.”**  
3. **“All tiers include unlimited storage” vs. “Maximum file size is 2 GB for Free users and 10 GB for paid users.”** (unlimited total storage but per‑file size caps)
